2023 — Resigned to It

December 2023. Canadians stop spiking with anger and start sustaining it. The crisis is no longer news — it's the background of daily life.

Spring 2024 — Peak Frustration

"The system is broken and no one is fixing it."

44.6%

April and June 2024: the highest negative sentiment in the entire 4-year dataset. One in every 2.2 records expresses distress or frustration.
